Wednesday May 16, 2001

tuned Griffith 4.0 wins SSR11 All-Night II in Proper game

In the proper game (Maybe here) I purchased and tuned (no race-mod) a Griffith 4.0 so I could use it to win both the Mega Speed Cup and (even without race-mod) the UKvsUS Series.

I've not run the All-nightII very often at all--perhaps because it has only one prize.

I decided the Griffith was a type of car I had never entered in this event, and so decided to use it.

It will also make good practice for the Abnormal Series (Tuned Series) I intend to enter the same car in next.

I had a good start and got away to a good lead right away.

I lapped the poor Civic on lap 9.

I had lapped the field by my one and only pit-stop, although the Supra managed to briefly unlap itself.

I had only one complete spin-out, on lap 27, but apart from that I had no time-consuming mishaps at all.

This was a very reasonably time for me in this race, but a full two minutes behind what a cheating GTO'95MR can pull off.

I won Cr150,000, and the silver Nismo GT-R LM.
