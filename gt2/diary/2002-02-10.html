Sunday February 10, 2002

Poor [R]GTO'92 SR Only Manages Third in the Grand Valley 300

I needed to test a used controller I bought, and so played a little more GT1. I did it in my main game (Maybe here)

I was surprised that I re-adapted to Gran Turismo almost sub-consciously.

However, I could not make the naturally-aspirated 1992 Mitsubishi GTO (sometimes known as the SR) go quite fast enough to win the Grand Valley 300.

I got a so-so start, and never got the lead.

I was in 3rd most of the race. I did manage to pass the Impreza to take second on my first shift, but lost control a little on lap 10 as it slipped by, and I never really caught it after that.

I kept third place on my first pit stop, and during that next shift regained second place when the Impreza pitted, but I dropped to third on my next pit stop and stayed in that position for the rest of the race.

Right at the end of the race the Skyline actually lapped me.
