In Fiji Game, [R]Camaro 30th Anniversary Edition Cleanly Wins the All-Night I

In the fiji game (Maybe here) Since I had never before entered a Camaro in the Endurance Races, and I had race-modified one for the UKvsUS, I entered it in the SSR11 All-Night I Race.

It won easily, but not spectacularly.

I had a scrappy start, but still got the lead by the middle of the first lap, and never lost it after that.

By my second stop I had just lapped the Castrol Supra.

I clipped the chicanes a couple of times, but had no serious mishaps. In fact, it was a very dull race.

I won Cr150,000, and a red Silvia LM.
