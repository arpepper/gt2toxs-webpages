Tue Jun 15, 1999

Utilizing Procedures for Getting International Prize Cars

I started playing the "alternate" game which previously won me the yellow ConceptCar. Here's what happened.

   
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Day(s)           Car         Event                Prize Car
    351      purpConceptCar    USvsJP sweep45to30    black FTO LM!?!
    357      [R]GTO'95MR       GT-I q4TM2nd,DFRW2nd
                                 1st 48to37          credits 
    364      [R]GTO'95MR       USvsJP sweep45to30    black FTO LM!?!
    370      [R]GTO'95MR       USvsJP sweep45to30    Viper w. green stripes!!!
    376      [R]GTO'95MR       USvsJP sweep45to30    green FTO LM!!!!!!
    382
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

So that quite quickly got the complete set of USvsJP prize cars.

The only surprise was getting a black FTO LM with the purple ConceptCar which previously only seemed to win white Vipers with blue stripes. In theory I should have persisted with the purple ConceptCar, but since I expected it to bring repeats, that is very difficult to do. I have kept a save of day 351 on a MegaMemory card, so I might go back and try several times with that car, as well as trying the GTO'95MR without first winning GT-I with it.

It could be, you know, that "GTO'95MR" is all the magic you need to get all the prize cars from the USvsJP series.
