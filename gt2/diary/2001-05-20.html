In Proper Game, Normal Prize [R]Impreza STi Wins GV300

In the proper game (Maybe here) I decided to use the race-modified Subaru Impreza WRX-STi Version III (Normal Prize) in the Grand Valley 300.

It was, as might be expected, a very easy win.

I basically zipped into the lead, although I had to jostle with the Skyline a little bit on the first lap.

I lapped the Lantis on lap 8.

The race was mostly uneventful, although I did lose control at the first hairpin once, after trying to pass the NSX on the outside going into it. That was probably lap 43.

The biggest excitement came when I had to alter my pit-stop strategy after accidentally pitting on lap 30. Based on my pre-race testing, I decided I could stretch the shifts out and make only one more pit-stop.

At the finish I was just about to lap the Skyline yet another time.

I won Cr300,000, and a black/blue Castrol Supra GT.

My time was my fastest Impreza time to date, beating the Rally Edition and the 1994 Wagon.

Note that my second half time was faster than my first, although I think I would have been a little bit faster if I had stuck to my original pit strategy of four evenly-spaced stops.
