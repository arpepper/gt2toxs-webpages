Friday June 09, 2000

I Finally Get ALL GOLDS on my B License!

Well, it was generally much easier to get all golds on my B license tests in GT2 than it had been in GT1.

It took me only three days once I put my mind to it. Here are my progress notes from my <html><a href="logs/gt2-game1">game log</a>. 

June 07, 2000
 643      license B-7 20.729G, B-9 23.532G          12G, 43S, 5B, 0K
 644   power off

June 08, 2000
 644      license B-8 23.270G
 645      license B-6 24.640G  (tried B-10, better S, but no G)
 646      license B-1 38.603G (AT) (tried B-4, but no good)
 647      license B-2 29.239G (AT)
 648      license B-5 32.410G (AT)
 649      license ?
 650      license B-4 23.655G (AT + digital)
 651      license try B-10, records, but no G
 652   power off

June 09, 2000
 652-654  license B-10 20.269G (AT, tried digital gas, but rejected)

Note that in order to pass B-4 (the small circle pad with the econobox) I had to use digital throttle/brake instead of my usual analog. This leads me to wonder quite strongly whether the analog throttle is incapable of getting up to 100%.

The Spoon S2000 is, as everyone says, a nice car. I won a yellow one.
