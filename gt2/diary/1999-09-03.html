I got a very good start, almost immediately grabbing the lead, and lapping my first three cars by my first pit stop at lap 12. I first lapped the entire field by about lap 15.

I had a near spinout on lap 33, and another one later, but no major disasters.

It was disappointing to have such a weak field against such a strong car, but it probably made little difference. It was easier to pass these competitors, but I needed to do it more often. Overall, the weak field probably does help slightly; the faster cars tend to slow you down a little more as you attempt to lap them.

As in a previous race, the Impreza and the Primera jostled, exchanging positions after I had finished, but they did not finish as close together this time.

I only just beat the most recent time I set with the Impreza Rally Edition (1-36:00.2), and I did not have any kitten interference this time. So, overall I would say that the two cars are very nearly equal.

I won Cr300,000, and a black/green Castrol Supra GT.
