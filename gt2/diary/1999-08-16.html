I got a reasonably good start; the slow field made it easy to get the lead by the end of the first lap. I led from lap 2 to the end, lapping the field for the first time by lap 40, and for good a few laps later.

I basically drove a steady race, but did get tired near then end and have a few minor disasters and slow laps then. Although my time could have beaten a fast field, the extra pressure of a fast field might have reduced my time somewhat, since this is not a good car for driving when nervous.

I won Cr300,000, and a black/blue Castrol Supra GT.
