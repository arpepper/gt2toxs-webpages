In Lancer Game, [R]Lancer EvoIV Cleanly Wins Special Stage R11 All-Night I

Oops. I accidentally used automatic transmission during qualifying. Oh well, I managed to com first anyway.

I got the lead by the first corner, and a couple of good laps immediately after that gave me a solid lead, out of the traffic which can slow you down so much at this course.

I lapped the Lantis before my first pit stop.

I eventually lapped the field (the GTO) on lap 19.

I almost lapped the Supra a second time, but didn't quite manage it, and had some bad laps times in the process. In general, I had quite a few mishaps in this race when I encountered traffic.

I won Cr150,000, and a red Silvia LM.
