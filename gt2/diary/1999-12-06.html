I led from start to finish again, in a fairly uneventful race.

This was about a minute slower than the Concept Car, but I still lapped the field three times.

I lapped the first car on lap 7, the second on lap 8, just before my first pit stop, but lapped the field by lap 11 or 12.

I had lapped them all a second time before my 2nd pit stop, and finally lapped them all a third time by lap 28.

I won Cr150,000, and the green Silvia LM. The green one looks so much nicer than the red one.
