In Nissan Game, [R]R32GT-R'89 Shatters All-Night I

After finishing the nissan game (Maybe here) I contined it beyond day 101, using the Nissan R32 Skyline GT-R '89 to try and set records, and so entered the race-mod version in the SSR11 All-Night I Race.

It won spectacularly, setting a personal record for this event. (Although I think I can better it by about another minute if I tune better and practise more).

After qualifying first, I never lost the lead.

I lapped my first cars on lap 5, and lapped the entire field by lap 7.

I lapped the field a second time on lap 15, and had some minor mishaps in the race, especially in traffic.

On lap 23 I lapped the field a third time, and just barely lapped the GTO a fourth time on the final lap.

It was a relatively dull race, notable only for the fact that I could not get my lap times down to quite what I wanted. I seemed to exhibit a lot of sloppiness in the central chicane.

I won Cr150,000, and a green Silvia LM.
