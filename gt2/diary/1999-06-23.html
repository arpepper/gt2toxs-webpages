I led from start to finish, even with pit stops.

I first lapped the sixth-place car on lap 12, and first lapped the lead on lap 30.

I had one semi-major disaster while passing, and lapped the leaders only once.

My time was worse than I had previously done with this car, but I had not practised in any other races at all.
