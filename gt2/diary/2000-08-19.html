Nismo 400R Does Reasonably Well in SSR11 All-Night II in Nismo Game

I took the tuned version of the 400R in my nismo game to the All-Night II race as a warm-up for the Tuned Car Series. It had already won the Mega-Speed Event, but that was a long time ago.

This car did a reliable job of winning the All-nightII.

I won Cr150,000, and the silver Nismo GT-R LM.
