The following are somewhat detailed text file logs I have kept for several Gran Turismo 1 games:

  • main main - A pseudo-log of my main game. I got tired of not knowing what I had done, so faked this log--real records begin on day 5446! That garage right now.
  • blue blue - I got all race prize cars in 1167 game days. That garage when finished.
  • allstars allstars - Branching off from my main game, I began collecting the stars from various "fast" games I had done. That garage so far.
  • silver silver - I got all race trophies reasonably quickly, in 132 game days. That garage when finished.
  • gold gold - I got all race trophies in the minimum possible time of 100 game days, ending on day 101. That garage when finished.
  • platinum platinum - Branching from the gold game, I try for all race trophies in the minimum possible time of 100 game days, ending on day 101, but buying even fewer cars. That garage when finished.
  • hastings hastings - The legend of Ray Hastings, who used a 1967 Corvette Stingray as his starter car. With luck, no other cars need be bought, but if one is unlucky, no 4wd will be won, necessitating a purchase. That garage when finished.
  • nismo nismo - I use a Nissan Nismo 400R as a starter car. You get this for all golds on your IA license (but I didn't...). In spite of getting Gran Turismo 2, I have gone back and finished this game. That garage so far.
  • fairlady fairlady - I use the Nissan Fairlady Z Type S Twin Turbo 2 Seater as much as possible in this minimal (100 day) game. That garage when finished.
  • fiji fiji - I started with the 4WD Fiji Blue Mitsubishi GTO'92 in this minimal (100 day) game. Admitting that I am not a great player, I planned on buying a Normal car entry. Using careful strategy I was able to buy a Griffith; more expensive than the Viper RT/10--the cheapest car capable of dominating the Normal Series. I cloned the prize Camaro as an entry for the UKvsUS Series, but in theory I could have modified the single car and still kept it technically eligible for the FR Series. After completing this game, I played a few more days of it experimenting for the proper game I later did. That garage when done.
  • lancer lancer - I got three Lancer Evolution IV GSR's from the 4WD seriers, and used them as much as possible. That garage when done.
  • proper proper - I finished this minimal (100 day) game in license-test order, selling prize cars. I also used Nissans as much as possible. That garage when finished.
  • nissan nissan - I finished this minimal (100 day) game in license-test order.
  • supra supra - I use the 1995 Toyota Supra RZ as much as possible in this minimal (100 day) game. In fact, I used Toyota for all races, except for the UKvsUS series. After that I went on to try out various other cars, experimenting with possible strategies, as well as just having fun. That garage when finished.
  • gt1-24hour gt1-24hour - I tried to do this game quickly, in terms of real time. It is not a minimal game for several reasons. I tried to finish in one 24-hour period, but did not quite manage that. That garage when finished.
  • gt1-mazda gt1-mazda - I started this as a minimal (100 day) game. I used Mazdas as much as possible. After completing it, I continued to collect all the cheapest used cars available. After making checkpoint saves, I race-modified the cheapest instance of each used car, and tried every one of them out in at least one of the GTI (World Cup), USvsJP, UKvsJP or GT Cup series. That garage when finished.
  • gt1-tuned gt1-tuned - In this offshoot of the gt1-mazda game, I began entering every model of used car in the Hard-Tuned Series. That garage so far.
  • gt1-2014 gt1-2014 - In 2014 I decided to start over again; a brand-new game. Sort of a 15th Anniversary celebration. That garage so far.
  • gt1-2019 gt1-2019 - In 2019, on the Victoria Day weekend, I decided to start over again; a brand-new game. I'd just received some new (refurbished) PS1 memory cards from Australia. Being right-hand drive, these memory cards take some getting used to. That garage so far.
  • starter starter - This is merely all three licenses, ending at day 25. By resetting the game on failed attempts, of course. Since I bought no cars up till then, this is a very boring garage... That garage when finished.
