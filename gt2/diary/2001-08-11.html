Tuned Silvia '88 K's wins SSR11 All-Night II in Nissan game

In the nissan game (Maybe here)

I am using the Silvia K's 1800cc, in various forms of modification, for as many events as possible, and so entered it in the All-Night II, partly to practise for the somewhat daunting Abnormal Series (Tuned Series) I intend to enter it in next.

Compared to some times I enter this race, I practised quite a lot, and it helped a bit, but I still found the car a little bit of a handful. It doesn't generally get wildly out of control, but it is difficult to keep it going exactly where and how you want.

One problem is that the suspension is very soft.

I initially lost my lead because of poor acceleration, but gained the lead back before the central chicane, and held it through it even though the RX-7 was right behind me.

By my first pitstop I had almost, but not quite, lapped the Civic. I kept my lead, however, and lapped the Civic soon after that.

Laps 17 and 18 were quite slow as I was attempting to lap two more cars.

I finally lapped the Supra on lap 25.

I had several mishaps in the central chicane, since this car does have some peculiar handling characteristics.

My time was not spectacular, but it's pretty good for a starter car, don't you think?

I won Cr150,000, and the silver Nismo GT-R LM.
