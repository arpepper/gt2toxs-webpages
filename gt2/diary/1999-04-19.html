Impressions of the FTO LM Edition

Well, yes it is an easy drive to win the Grand Valley 300, and certainly has adequate speed, etc., but it isn't all that much fun to drive. It handles a little like a truck. A very fast truck, but a truck. It's acceleration, while consistent, is always somewhat gradual. The car sticks well on corners, but is difficult to provoke into corrective maneuvers. On the other hand, because it corners predictably, corrective measures are not often necessary.
