In Fairlady Game, [R]Fairlady Z Turbo Cleanly Wins Grand Valley 300

In the fairlady game (Maybe here) I used the Fairlady Z 2 seater Turbo (aka 300ZX) as much as possible, so I entered the Grand Valley 300 with the race-mod version.

It won reasonably convincingly, although not spectacularly by my current standards.

I got the lead by the end of the first lap, and that was it!

I lapped the Accord by lap 11.

At my first pit stop, I had a good lead, and never lost it.

By my second stop, I had lapped the Impreza (it didn't quite stick), and therefore almost lapped the field. I actually lapped the field when I passed the GT-R on lap 30.

At my fourth and final stop, I had just passed the GT-R again, although the pass didn't stick.

I eventually passed the GT-R again on lap 53.

I almost lapped the Impreza another time on lap 59, but I spun on the first corner and consequently put in that disastrously slow lap.

I had one other minor mishap in the race, when the car pulled outwards from the line into the second hairpin, but I recovered that one losing only a couple of seconds.

I won Cr300,000, and a black/blue Castrol Supra GT.
