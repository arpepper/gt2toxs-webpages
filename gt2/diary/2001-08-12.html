In Nissan Game, [R]Silvia'88 K's Wins All-Night I

In the nissan game (Maybe here) I used the race-mod version of the 1988 S13 Silvia K's 1800cc in the SSR11 All-Night I Race.

It won spectacularly, although about a minute short of a personal record for this event. (Although I think I can better it by about another two minutes yet with a monster car).

I dropped back slightly at the start, but got the lead back by the central chicane of the first lap.

By my first stop I had lapped two cars (just). I lapped the field on lap 14.

By my second stop I had lapped 2 of the cars yet again, and was way in the lead.

On lap 26 I lapped the field a second time, and also lapped the Lantis for a third time.

On lap 30 I just barely lapped the EXIV a third time.

It was another very dull race. I didn't even have any noteable mishaps. This time was relatively good, but perhaps only because I have not done this race much since I learned to drive fast. I'll probably try a Skyline soon.

I won Cr150,000, and a red Silvia LM.
