But I buy cars from myself in Gran Turismo all the time...

Those LM prize cars are a real bargain at only Cr35000. That way I can have one to leave stock, and one to mess up with my fiddling. I don't trust those stupid "settings" files, besides you can't just read them.

I wonder. Is it ethical to artificially run a GT game up to day 60 to buy a bargain, and sell that back to a day 1 game? Oh well, you can always get an MA70 Supra for < Cr8000 and that's the only car you need to get the game rolling, since (modified) it can win the GT Cup.

See, I'm co-operative with a slightly devious mind.

Oh, you might want to pay a little more than Cr8000 to get a red one, since red cars are always the best.
