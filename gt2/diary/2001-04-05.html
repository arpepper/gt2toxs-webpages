In Fairlady Game, [R]Fairlady Z Turbo Cleanly Wins the All-Night I

In the fairlady game (Maybe here) I used the Fairlady Z 2 seater Turbo (aka 300ZX) as much as possible, so I entered the SSR11 All-Night I Race with the race-mod version.

It won reasonably convincingly, although not spectacularly by my current standards.

I had a scrappy first lap, and so crossed the line in third place.

By the first corner of the second lap, however, I had gained first place.

I lapped my first car on lap 12, and lapped the field on lap 17. I ended up lapping all but the Skyline twice, and must have lapped the Lancer three times.

I clipped the chicanes a couple of times, but had no real wipeouts.

Surprisingly, the time was almost identical to that I did with the Lancer.

I won Cr150,000, and a red Silvia LM.
