Race-modded Cerbera Wins at Grand Valley

I had one unusual mishap, and a couple of minor ones.

I just lapped the GT-R LM one more time in the last lap.

I recorded lots of lap times of 1:35.XXX

I won Cr300,000, and a black/blue Castrol Supra GT.

My time was a lot better than I previously managed with this car. That somewhat made up for the disappointment of not getting the second prize car in the second attempt at this race.
