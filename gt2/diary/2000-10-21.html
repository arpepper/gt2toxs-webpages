Lowish-powered FTO GP Version R Cleanly Wins Grand Valley 300 Against a Strong Field

I dropped to fifth off the line, but soon began battling the GT-R for 4th place, getting clear into 4th place by the end of lap 2. I then began challenging for third place, getting that by the end of lap 4, and moving up to the lead by lap 5 or 6.

My tires lasted very well, so I made only two stops, and hardly saw the rest of the field after that, except when I began lapping them around the time of my last pit stop.

One thing I noticed was that this car can catch a draft and get extra speed very well. That is why I set the fastest lap on lap 3, while chasing the GT-R LM.

I won Cr300,000, and a black/blue Castrol Supra GT.

This time was almost as fast as my time for the same race the first time I entered the FTO LM Edition.
