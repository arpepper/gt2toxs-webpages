tuned GT-R'89 Demolishes SSR11 All-Night II in Nissan game

In the nissan game (Maybe here), I am now using the GT-R'89 in as many events as possible. So I entered it in the All-Night II, hoping to set a new record, and possibly to break 50 minutes total time for the event.

I lost the lead briefly at the start, but gained it back before the first corner, and never lost it again.

I lapped the entire field on lap 7, and stop 1 on lap 10 was uneventful.

I lapped the field again by lap 14, and had lapped most if not all cars yet a third time by my second stop on lap 20, although a of those cars passed me in the pits.

Traffic held me up only slightly in this car. I probably lapped the field for the fourth time on lap 28, and continued to lap a few cars another time after that.

I had no serious mishaps, although I did get a little bit out-of-shape occasionally on some parts of the course, especially in the central chicane.

My time was my best so far, but I still think I ought to be able to break 50 minutes, and want to try again to do so sometime.

I won Cr150,000, and the silver Nismo GT-R LM.
