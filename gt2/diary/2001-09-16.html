In Mitsubishi Game, [R]GTO'92 twinturbo Convincingly Wins Grand Valley 300

In the mitsu game (Maybe here) I am using the 1992 Mitsubishi GTO twin turbo as much as possible, and so entered the race-mod version in the Grand Valley 300.

It was a very easy win.

I got a good start, and never lost the lead.

I lapped the entire field shortly after my first stop on lap 12.

I kept going at that progress, and had just lapped the GTO a fourth time but it did not stick, and, although I lapped it again soon after that, I failed to lap either the GTO or the NSX a fifth time.

I won Cr300,000, and a black/blue Castrol Supra GT.

I beat my recent Skyline GT-R'89 time by 12 seconds, and my Cerbera LM time by 8 seconds, so this is currently my second fastest car. However, during this race I did not run into the pitstop bug, so that more than accounts for why the Skyline was slower.
