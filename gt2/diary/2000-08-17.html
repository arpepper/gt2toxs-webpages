Nismo 400R Does Reasonably Well in SSR11 All-Night I in Nismo Game

I decided to go back to my old GT1 nismo game. In this game I had been using the Nismo 400R as much as possible, so I entered the race-mod version in the All-night I Event, and did a reasonably fast time. I was surprised, actually, that playing GT2 actually seems to have improved my GT1 skills.
