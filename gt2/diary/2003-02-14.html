tuned FDRX7'91 Wins SSR11 All-Night II in mazda game

In the mazda game (Maybe here), I used the 1991 FD RX-7 efini Type R for many races.

So I decided to enter the hard-tuned version in the All-Night I.

I started from pole position, got a good start, and never lost the lead.

I began lapping the cars on lap 12, starting by passing the pokey Civic.

I had a very fast second shift, with a mediocre first and last shift. I never seemed to keep as consistently fast as practice suggested I could.

I used much stiffer stabilizers than I had in the tuned series, or on the race-mod version, but perhaps they were too stiff, since I got violent pulling to one side occasionally.

I won Cr150,000, and the silver Nismo GT-R LM.
