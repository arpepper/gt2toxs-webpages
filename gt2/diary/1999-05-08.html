[R]Viper GTS far outperforms Viper GTS-R at Grand Valley 300

I had expected the race-modded red Viper GTS to be much easier to drive and much faster than its supposedly superior cousin, the GTS-R, and I was not disappointed.

Consistency was the key here. Compare the times to the Supra RZ, for instance. The Supra had some faster lap times, but less consistency, and so was slightly slower overall.

I got off to a rasonable start for once, getting the lead by the end of the first lap. I lapped the last-place car just before my first pit stop, and never lost the lead at all.

The average lap time (as evidenced by reading the finishing time) was a lot closer to the fastest lap time for this car than for any other. As far as I can remember, I had no disasters at all!

I did not do many 1:35 laps, but a lot of 1:36's.

I won Cr300,000, and a black/green Castrol Supra GT.

After the race, I tried a time trial, and managed 3:15.616 for two laps. My first lap was almost identical to my qualifying time.

I then took the ConceptCar out, and (eventually; it is very difficult to get used to) did 1:34.427 + 1:28.998 = 3:03.425; once or twice I had done a 1:33.xxx first lap, too.

I then took the race-modded GTO'95 MR out, since it is the next candidate for the Grand Valley 300. I tried hard, and eventually beat its existing 3:16.xxx ghost record. Then I switched to the normal intercooler, and going fast was much easier and I soon did 3:13.939; later I was to discover that the top gear needed reducing in order to allow acceleration.

Anyway, for a quick warm-up, I tried the 4wd Challenge, which I hadn't done in a while. Here are the results.
