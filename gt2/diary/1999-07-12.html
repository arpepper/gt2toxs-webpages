Would you believe I collected the race prize cars a second time?

<<-- Prize Car Patterns-->>

For the last two or three weeks I spent a lot of time completing a second game from scratch, in which I collected all the race prize cars in as short a time (in terms of simulation days) as I was able.

It took me longer than I expected, but today I eventually did finish.

A detailed log of this is at logs/blue

Significantly, I did not race any simulation races in the game, nor even do any time trials. You will notice I enter races redundantly in the superstitious (but not unfounded) belief that it helps "unlock" new prize cars.
