In Mitsu Game, [R]GTO'92twinturbo Wins All-Night I

In the mitsu game (Maybe here), I used Mitsubishi cars for as many races as possible, and used the GTO'92 (twinturbo, not SR) for most races.

So I decided to enter the race-mod version in the All-Night I, hoping to set a new record, and maybe to break 48 minutes total time for the event. Sadly, I didn't quite manage it.

It still won pretty spectacularly, coming close to my personal record for this event.

After qualifying first, a clean start put me into the lead right away and I never lost it.

I had lapped the entire field well before my first pit stop after lap 10.

Shortly after my second pit stop, after lap 20, I lapped the leaders again, but I think that must have been for the third time, and they must have been in the bits when I had previously lapped them for the second time. I didn't quite seem to lap the GTO again, so I think I only lapped it three times, though perhaps I lapped it a fourth time while it was in the pits.

I ran into the central chicane barriers once, but found several times, too, that traffic really slowed my lap times.

I won Cr150,000, and a green Silvia LM.
