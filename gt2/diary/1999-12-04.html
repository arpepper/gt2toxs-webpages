Saturday, December 04, 1999

I Start Another Game, starring Ray Hastings

After getting all golds on my B license, I wanted to get back to racing, so I started another "100 day" career.

I've called this game "Hastings", after the fictional character I imagine is doing the racing. You can view it, and a day-by-day garage, here.

Ray Hastings started his Gran Turismo Career by procuring a 1967 Corvette Stingray, Rally red in colour, for Cr8,000, from an unknown source.

The first thing he did, after getting all his licenses was to sweep the Normal Series with this beast.
