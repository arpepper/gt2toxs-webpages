I started from pole position, got a good start, and never lost the lead.

I began lapping the cars on lap 9, but had to repass most of those after my stop on lap 10. I'm not sure when I lapped the field.

I had lots of minor mishaps; my tuning was not great and the car pulled to the outside a bit under braking.

All-in-all this was a very mediocre run.

It's too bad I didn't use my own tuning advice from the last time I ran this race with this car.

I won Cr150,000, and the silver Nismo GT-R LM.
