tuned GTO'92 Comes Close to SSR11 All-Night II in Mitsu game

In the mitsu game (Maybe here), I used Mitsubishi cars for as many races as possible, and used the GTO'92 (twinturbo, not SR) for most races.

So I decided to enter the tuned version in the All-Night II, hoping to set a new record, and maybe to break 50 minutes total time for the event. Sadly, I didn't quite manage it.

I started from pole position, got a good start, and never lost the lead.

I lapped the entire field by my first pitstop on lap 8, although one of the cars went by me during the stop.

Traffic held me up only slightly in this car. I probably lapped the field for the fourth time close to the end, and continued to lap a few cars another time after that.

I had one messy spin on lap 5, going into the first chicane.

My time was 15 seconds slower than the Skyline GT-R'89, so perhaps I should try the GTO'95MR again soon for more direct comparison.

On the other hand, the 15 seconds could have been gained by taking only two pitstops, as the Skyline did, although I'm not 100% confident I would not have lost more time with slow laps.

I won Cr150,000, and the silver Nismo GT-R LM.
