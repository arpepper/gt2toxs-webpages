First off, I could have come second, but let the GT-R ahead on the last lap in order to confirm what 3rd place prize money was. No really!!!

I was in a solid second place until my first pit stop. Then I dropped to 4th, got to 1st in the exchange, but was raced into 2nd.

lap 20 - dropped to 3rd.

lap 30 - dropped from 1st to 3rd again.

On the road, I just barely got 1st by lap 40, but my pit stop put me in third.

My stop at lap 50 put me in a solid 2nd. No last-minute stop by the leader to put me in the lead this time...

<<-- Prize Money-->>

In fact, the third place car did begin challenging me, but I could have held him off. In fact, it was quite difficult letting him by for the last time. (Note my very slow last lap time).

I had several problems negotiating "the hairpin".

I won Cr150,000, and no car.

The next Grand Valley 300 entrant will be an encore performance by the ConceptCar. And, after that, I think I will take a rest.
