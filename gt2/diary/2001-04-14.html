In Supra Game, [R]Supra '95 RZ Dominates the All-Night I

In the supra game (Maybe here) I used the 1995 Supra RZ as much as possible, so I entered the SSR11 All-Night I Race with the race-mod version.

It won reasonably convincingly, although not spectacularly by my current standards.

I had a reasonable start, and was well in the lead by the end of the first lap.

I lapped my first car on lap 8, but it went by when I pitted.

By stop 2 I was way in the lead, and had probably lapped the field already.

I had 3 or 4 bad spins, since the handling of this, like other Supras it seems, is very sensitive to tire wear.

I won Cr150,000, and a red Silvia LM.
