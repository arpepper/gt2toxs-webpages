I Add A Search Facility to this Diary

While doing some work for a professor, I learned of a freeware Search Engine at http://www.worldwidemart.com/scripts/.

And so I used it to create this search facility for these diary pages. So now you have an easy way to search their entire content. In addition, you can still search for words in the full index, as you could before.

Because of that and various other things, I have regenerated all pages from their source. (To suit the Search Engine, I added the title of the page to the official title in the header; previously those titles used to be the date only).

GT2-wise I've been wasting a lot of time recently driving the Skyline R30 Silhouette Foumula[sic]. It is a ridiculously good car, capable of winning all the premier events, and, since it has only 555 bhp, can enter a lot of secondary events (such as the GT300 in which it annihilates the competition) as well. This car is the prize for winning the Tahiti Road Race of the 80's Series. You can win it in either red or blue.
