(GT2 Version 1.0)  GameShark Addresses for Lap Counters

Threads:
 Gran Turismo 2
 Gameplay Hints
 Game Shark Hints


On Thursday night I did the work to create the drag racing
code for Version 1.1 and 1.2

Along the way I discovered the lap counter locations for all the
cars in an AI race, in all versions.  Here they are for version
1.0

<HTML INDENT>
<tt><font face="Courier"><pre>
 $A99AC -- player
 $AA4EC -- AI starting 5th
 $AB02C -- AI starting 4th
 $ABB6C -- AI starting 3rd
 $AC6AC -- AI starting 2nd
 $AD1EC -- AI starting 1st
</pre></font></tt>
</HTML>
