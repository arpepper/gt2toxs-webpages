In Lancer Game, [R]Lancer EvoIV Cleanly Wins Grand Valley 300

In the lancer game I used the Lancer Evolution IV GSR (4WD Series prize) as much as possible, so I entered the race-modified version in the Grand Valley 300.

I got the lead by the first corner, and the race was effectively over. The field was not super-fast, but was reasonably fast, and my winning time was sufficient to beat (and lap) any field I have ever seen.

Not only that, I beat another Lancer Evolution IV GSR by several laps. I even used the same horsepower.

At my first pit stop, I had a good lead, and never lost the lead, and I lapped the Impreza (not to mention the Accord and Lancer) before half-way, although the Impreza did get by me while I was in the pits.

At my second stop, I was actually beginning to lap the front of the field, but in fact I lapped the Supra by about lap 40, shortly before my third stop.

I won Cr300,000, and a black/blue Castrol Supra GT.
