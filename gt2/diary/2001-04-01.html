tuned Fairlady Z 2 seater Turbo wins SSR11 All-Night II in fairlady game

In the fairlady game (Maybe here) I used the Fairlady Z 2 seater Turbo (aka 300ZX) as much as possible, so I entered the All-nightII Race.

This car won the All-nightII, but not very convincingly.

Well, qualifying 6th was a bad omen, but at least I won.

I had a terrible start and an extraordinarily bad first lap, but clawed my way back after that, regaining the lead about lap 3.

At my first stop, I dropped briefly into second place, but not for long. I kept my lead at my second stop.

I lapped one car on lap 29, but even it unlapped itself on lap 30.

I had one bad spin, and a couple of other mess-ups.

But at least I did had cleanly won the Hard-Tuned Series with the same car before this.

I won Cr150,000, and the silver Nismo GT-R LM.
