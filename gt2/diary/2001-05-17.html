In Proper Game, [R]Impreza STi III Dominates the All-Night I

In the proper game (Maybe here) I was using the race-modified version of the Normal prize Impreza STi III a lot, since I had never tried it before, and it seemed like a very nice car.

So I entered it in the SSR11 All-Night I Race.

It won spectacularly, setting a personal record for this event.

I had a good start, and drew quickly away from the field.

I lapped the field by lap 8, and stayed barely ahead of the second-place car as I came out of my first pit-stop.

On lap 18 I lapped the field twice, and lap 27 I lapped it a third time.

It was another very dull race. I didn't even have any noteable mishaps. This is actually the fastest I have ever done this race, although I should be able to go a lot faster yet if I use a high-powered 4WD (e.g. GTO) or the Concept Car.

I won Cr150,000, and a red Silvia LM.
