(GT2 Version 1.0) GameShark Addresses for Lap Counters

On Thursday night I did the work to create the drag racing code for Version 1.1 and 1.2

Along the way I discovered the lap counter locations for all the cars in an AI race, in all versions. Here they are for version 1.0 

 $A99AC -- player
 $AA4EC -- AI starting 5th
 $AB02C -- AI starting 4th
 $ABB6C -- AI starting 3rd
 $AC6AC -- AI starting 2nd
 $AD1EC -- AI starting 1st
