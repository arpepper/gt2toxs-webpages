tuned 1995 Supra RZ wins SSR11 All-Night II in Supra game

In the supra game (Maybe here) I used the 1995 Toyota Supra RZ as much as possible, so I entered the All-nightII Race.

This car won the All-nightII, but again, not very convincingly.

I had a good start and got away to a good lead.

At my first stop, I kept my lead, but had a couple of very bad laps during the next shift.

I almost lapped the Civic on lap 15, and finally passed it on lap 17, eventually lapping it another time close to the end of the race.

Primaily because of the slow field, I lapped several cars on the last lap.

I had one several bad spins.

I won Cr150,000, and the silver Nismo GT-R LM.
