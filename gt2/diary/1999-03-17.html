Another Level of Crash Bandicoot 3

Monday and Tuesday night I redid FR Series and UKvsJP with a Cerbera LM. With that car I can leave the UKvsJP competitors behind, and can leave the FR Series waaay behind,

Earlier Tuesday evening my nephew was over with his CD and memory card for Crash Bandicoot 3. Turns out there was another motorcycle racing level he and his mother wanted me to try. This one was harder; it took me five or six tries to complete, maybe even more.
