Friday November 03, 2000

I drive race-mod Subaru '94 Wagon WRX to an Easy Win in a Sharked 60 lap High Sppeed Ring Enduro

I've always wanted to do an Enduro at the High Speed Ring, and now, thanks to PotHole, I've managed to do it.

I chose my trusty 1994 Subaru Impreza Wagon WRX as a possible compromise between way too each, and a little too challenging for my first test. Perhaps it was a little too easy, actually.

I dropped behind the Twinturbo for half a lap, but then I got by at the esses, and that was that.

I lapped the Lantis very soon, on lap 8.

I was in the process of lapping the field by my first stop (although the Supra was still a little way ahead, albeit a lap down, at that point). Eventually I lapped the entire field once.

I won Cr300,000, and a black/blue Castrol Supra GT.
