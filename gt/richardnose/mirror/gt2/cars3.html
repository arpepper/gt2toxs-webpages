Also Known As

This shows alternate names for the car. In most cases this is the name used in Replays and the Trade Car list. In some cases it gives the name shown on the car's screen in the garage.
The names given here are from the PAL version and may differ from the Japanese and North American versions.

Colours

Yes colours (Im not an american!!!)
While colours dont affect performance (but red has been proven to be quicker), some people like to collect cars in all the colours combinations. This colour list is possibly not complete, as it has been done without a GameShark, by simply racing and winning the cars.
Some prize cars (the Yaris, Lupo and Clio) are available from the dealer, so the colours are listed there. A few prize cars (eg ZZII) are listed, but not for sale, from the Special section of their dealer. Again this shows the colours available.
For most others, it is simply a case of win the car and check the colour. Just be careful as some cars come in shades of the same colour, although these have the name given, so read the name! Even for cars without a named colour it is easily possible to confuse silver and white!

Good luck with them - and email me if you find any I dont have list.
