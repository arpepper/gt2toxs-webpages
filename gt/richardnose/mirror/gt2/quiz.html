Gran Turismo 2 Quiz

This is a quiz to see how much trivia you know about GT2.

A lot of the answers can be found on my website, but all of them can be found by playing GT2, although some will require reading the fine print!

These are based on the PAL version, so if any of the questions have different answers in the other versions, Id love to know about it.

Easy

Some easy ones to warm up with...

  1. Which manufacturer has just 1 car on offer?
  2. Which car has 2 engines?
  3. What is the lightest car?
  4. Which Special race wins you a car that can be sold for $500k?
  5. Which Japanese manufacturer only has 4WD models in their new car section?
  6. Which supercar comes standard with only 3 gears?
  7. What is the least powerful car?
  8. In which Endurance race can you sometimes win a car that can be sold for $500k?
  9. Which drive type has no special race series?
  10. Which manufacturer event gets the grandiose title of "Festival of Speed"?

Medium

These are a bit harder...

  1. What is the only manufacturer event that requires a prize car to enter?
  2. Which special models have names that start with numbers?
  3. Which prize cars are available to be bought new from their manufacturer?
  4. What is the quickest race?
  5. Which manufacturer has no cars that can be race models?
  6. What is the most powerful normally aspirated prize car?
  7. In the Smokey Mountain North rally, which is the slowest computer car?
  8. Of the races on fixed tracks, which track has the most laps raced?
  9. Which prize car can be won from 2 different race series?
  10. Of the new tracks in GT2, which one doesnt get a race with 2 laps?

Hard

Into the tough questions. You will have to have dug around in GT2 to get these.
  1. Which Japanese new cars come with a 7 speed gearbox?
  2. Which car has the most torque?
  3. Which race occasionally has a Shelby Cobra Coupe race car hidden amongst the non race cars?
  4. What special model race car can be tuned for more power?
  5. What shade of yellow does the B gold prize, the Spoon S2000, come in?
  6. Which prize car has the best power to weight ratio for cars eligible for 592hp races?
  7. Which car develops its peak power at the highest revs?
  8. Which manufacturer sells some special models without tyres or other parts?
  9. Which prize cars when they are race modified offer 2 types of race colour schemes?
  10. Which special model car has no stabilizer adjustment in its customized suspension?
