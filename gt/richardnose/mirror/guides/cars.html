Car Comparison

There are many car comparisons available, but this one includes some extra information that really helps to show why some cars are so good when their power and weight don't show the strong points of the car.

In particular, the ride height and downforce help to show cars that are "glued" to the road. Front downforce is a major factor in how well a car can corner at high speed. This varies some what and explains the strength of many of the Special edition cars.

The revs for max power and max torque also give a good idea of the powerband of the car. The further apart these revs, the fatter the powerband, the closer these two rev values are, the narrower the powerband..
However, these are not fool proof, some have the same revs, but actually have a wide powerband. See Power and Torque Curves Guide for more details.

Won - Normal Cars

These are all non race modified prize cars, that have then been fully modified with tuning and a race body. This shows the full potential of these cars.

In some cases the car could have both NA tuning and a turbo kit. Both can be fitted, but only one or the other can be used at the same time. For these both tunings are listed.

The tuning shows both the type and the maximum tune allowed. This helps indicate the cost to tune the car to this level.

Note: many of these cars differ from the same versions that can be bought. Some differ straight off, others don't differ until fully tuned.
e.g. the Supra RZ is only 897HP, instead of 910 for a bought version.
