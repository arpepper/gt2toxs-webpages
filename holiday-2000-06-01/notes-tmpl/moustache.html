Race Prep, Step 2: Just a Moustache!

This is another intermediate picture in the process of getting to the stage of being clean-shaven, except for sort-of Richard Petty 70's style sideburns.

My wife had gone to bed already by this time, so I used the timer in the camera to take the picture. This is why the top of my head is cut off; I simply put the camera on a counter in the kitchen, and aimed it only roughly. It also explains why I forgot to put anything over the undershirt, since I had removed my shirt to prevent messing it up while shaving, and my wife was not there to remind me to put it on again.
